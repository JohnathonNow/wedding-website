Our Story

December 2012

The story begins with Breãnna making the difficult decision to move across the country from a cow pasture in rural Northern California to the "big city" of South Bend, Indiana to attend college at the University of Notre Dame. She planned on majoring in Math.

December 2013

Similarly, a year later John made the same hard decision to leave his family, to also attend the University of Notre Dame, planning to major in Computer Science.

Breãnna came to her senses and switched her major to Computer Science.

March 2016

Breãnna got together with a few of her friends to re-up the Notre Dame Linux Users Group.

John, urged by one of the co-founders of the club, joined the club Slack channel and started annoying Breãnna, much to her chagrin. Apparently, they were also in the same Stats class.

Later that month, John finally shows his face to a club meeting, after Breãnna made the very good point that it was so easy for him to annoy her online, but avoid her in person. She was fully expecting to despise him in person, but once they met, she knew they were going to be good friends.

August 2016

Their friendship continued, and at the end of the summer when John and Breãnna came back to campus, all of a sudden they started being "shipped" by Pbui, who they both had become good friends with. It probably started out as a very not-subtle joke, but it caused John and Breãnna to become even better friends.

October 2016

Joke's on Pbui, because John and Breãnna actually started dating.

Our Story

December 2012

The story begins with Breãnna making the really difficult decision to move across the country from a cow pasture in rural Northern California to the "big city" of South Bend, Indiana to attend college at the University of Notre Dame. Although 17-year-old Breãnna was unaware of how poor this decision would turn out to be, she was planning on working towards a dual degree in Honors Math and Electrical Engineering.

December 2013

Similarly, John made the same hard decision to leave his family, to also attend the University of Notre Dame, planning to major in Computer Science.

August 2014

John started his Freshman year at Notre Dame. Breãnna came to her senses, realizing her true calling was, in fact, to not be eternally in despair. She switched her major to Computer Science.

August 2014 - January 2016

The pair may have crossed paths over the next year and a half, but we will never know.

February 2016

Inspired by a notorious professor that she was TA-ing for, Breãnna got together with a few of her friends to re-up the Notre Dame Linux Users Group. John, urged by one of the co-founders of the club, joined the club Slack channel and started to harass Breãnna, much to her chagrin. Apparently, they were also in the same Stats class, though Breãnna rarely made an appearance to the course's lectures. Also, that class Breãnna was TA-ing? John was one of her students. Later that month, John finally shows his face to a club meeting, after Breãnna made the very good point that it was so easy for him to mock her online, but avoid her in person. She was fully expecting to dispise him in person, but once she saw him, she had the overwhelming feeling that she needed to befriend him, no matter how timid and hesitant he would be.

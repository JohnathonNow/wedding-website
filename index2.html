Site Map & Parking

From Lower State Road, turn onto Farm View Road to access the parking lots. Please park in the Upper (UP) or Main Parking (M) lots.

Terrain Gardens is building I with main entrance path at E.

Airports

The two closest airports are Philadelphia International Airport (PHL) and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). See below for train options from either airport.

Public Transportation

Terrain Gardens is located at Delaware Valley University, which is a stop on the Lansdale/Doylestown (LAN) SEPTA Regional Rail line. The Delaware Valley University station is a half mile walk to the venue, just follow Field Drive until Farm View Road, and you'll end up at the Upper Parking (UP) for the venue (top left in the Site Map above). This line comes from and goes to Center City Philadelphia.

From PHL, you can take the SEPTA Airport Line (AIR) to Center City and transfer at Suburban or Jefferson to the LAN line.

From EWR, you can take AirTrain Newark to the Newark Airport Rail Station. From there, take the Northeast Corridor Commuter Rail line to William H. Gray III 30th Street, and transfer to the LAN line.
